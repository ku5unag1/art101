Vanilla cake

  1. Gather baking equiptment:
    1. Cake mold
    2. Parchment Paper
    3. Mixer
    4. Mixing Bowls
    5. Measuring spoons and cups
  2. Gather baking materials:
    1. Cooking spray
    2. 2 ⅔ cups of all-purpose flour
    3. 1 cup of white sugar
    4. 1 tablespoon of baking powder
    5. 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
    6. Salt
    7. 3 eggs
    8. ¾ cups of milk
    9. ¾ cups of vegetable oil
  4. Preheat oven to 350℉.
  5. Spray the cake mold and line it with the parchment paper
  6. Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, vanilla extract and salt in a bowl
  7. To the same bowl, add the eggs, milk and vegetable oil
  8. Mix until smooth
  9. Pour the cake batter into the cake mold
  10. Bake the cake until a toothpick can be inserted into the center and come out without any residue. (Usually about 1 hour)
  11. Take out the cake and let it cool for 15-30 minuets
  12. Consume

Minesweeper

  1. Print minesweeper board
    1. Randomly generate bombs with each new map
  2. Accept user input
  3. Check if selected block has a bomb or not
    1. If there is a bomb, the game resets
  4. Clear out blocks based on input location
  5. Calculate how many bombs are within each block’s 3x3 vicinity and print on designated block
  6. If the remaining blocks are all bomb spaces, then the player wins and the game resets
